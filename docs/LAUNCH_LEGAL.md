# Launch legal notes

Internal checklist for Findings launch. Not legal advice.

## Product name: "Findings"

**Status:** Generic/common name. Multiple unrelated uses exist.

| Entity | Domain / use | Relevance |
|--------|----------------|-----------|
| Findings (Diginex) | findings.co | B2B vendor risk / compliance SaaS. Different market, but same software-adjacent name. |
| Jewelry "findings" | Various retailers | Unrelated industry term. |
| This product | Findings (public data analysis) | Descriptive/generic; harder to protect exclusively, lower immediate conflict risk in this niche. |

**Recommended before scaling branding:**

1. Run a formal search on [USPTO TESS](https://tmsearch.uspto.gov/) for "Findings" in Class 009 (software) and Class 042 (SaaS).
2. Check state business registries if you incorporate.
3. If traction grows, consult an IP attorney about registrability and coexistence strategy.
4. Terms page already notes the name may be used elsewhere.

**You do not need a registered trademark to launch.**

## Ongoing compliance

- Only ingest datasets with allowed licenses (enforced in API).
- Show `attribution_text` on results when users may publish findings.
- World Bank (CC BY 4.0): attribution required on reuse.
- FRED: cite series and link to FRED; comply with [FRED API terms](https://fred.stlouisfed.org/docs/api/fred/terms_of_use.html).
- Do not use third-party logos without permission.
- Do not claim official partnership with data.gov, Fed, World Bank, or NYC.
