# Licensing — strict allowlist

> Not legal advice. Have counsel review Terms and Privacy before scale.

## Allow ingest

- **CC0 1.0**
- **US Public Domain** / US Government Work (with portal policy check)
- **PDDL**

## Reject ingest

- CC-BY, CC-BY-SA, ODbL, NC, ND, unknown, custom restrictive terms

## UI provenance (always)

Per dataset: title, organization, link to original, license badge, date accessed.

Results footer: analysis on copy retrieved [date]; not affiliated with publisher.

## AI and redistribution

- Session copies for analysis only; no bulk download resale
- Document in Terms of Service

## Launch checklist

- [ ] License gate in code (no bypass)
- [ ] Terms of Service + Privacy Policy
- [ ] Source attribution on every session
- [ ] Takedown / mis-license contact
